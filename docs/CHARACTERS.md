## El Sacerdote Hueco *(The Hollow Priest)*

**Archetype:** Antagonist  
**Nightmare Level:** 3+

A keeper of ancient Teotl rites whose consciousness has been replaced by
something older. He appears in ritual spaces, speaking fragmented liturgy
and reversed Spanish. He never blinks. He is always aware of the player
even when facing away.

**Design Notes:**
- Tall, impossibly thin silhouette.
- Robes that move against the wind direction.
- Voice processed with extreme reverb and backwards masking.

## La Niña que Llora *(The Weeping Child)*

**Archetype:** Ambiguous  
**Nightmare Level:** 1+

Found in every location, never where expected. Long black hair obscures her
face entirely. She cries without making any sound. Approaching her triggers
environmental disturbances; ignoring her may be worse.

**Design Notes:**
- Small scale emphasises wrongness in large architectural spaces.
- Bare feet. Traditional dress, but decades out of context.
- Sobbing animation visible only in peripheral vision.

---

## El Otro Tú *(Mirror Self)*

**Archetype:** Reflection  
**Nightmare Level:** 4+

The player's own reflection, acting with independent agency. Occasionally
helpful (points toward exits, picks up items the player missed). Fundamentally
untrustworthy. Its actions have consequences the player cannot predict.

**Design Notes:**
- Identical player model with a half-second delay on all animations.
- Its shadow points in a different direction.
- Dialogue is the player's own internal monologue, spoken aloud.
