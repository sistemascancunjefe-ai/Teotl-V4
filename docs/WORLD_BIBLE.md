# World Bible — Teotl V4 · Nightmare Mode

> **Status:** Placeholder — expand with writer / narrative direction.

## Setting

Teotl V4 takes place in a liminal space between the waking world and the
nightmare realm — a crumbling pre-Columbian ritual complex that exists
outside normal time. The aesthetic blends **Mesoamerican architecture**
with **psychological body horror** and **glitch/digital corruption**.

## Tone & Themes

- **Psychological horror** over jump scares — dread built through atmosphere,
  unreliable perception, and environmental storytelling.
- **Memory and identity** — the player questions whether they are the protagonist
  or the entity they are hunting.
- **Ritual and transformation** — the Teotl rites as a metaphor for cycles of
  violence and renewal.

## World Rules

1. **The Nightmare Bleeds Through** — higher nightmare levels cause the world
   geometry to warp. Doorways move. Text changes when re-read.
2. **Nothing is Permanent** — items picked up may disappear. Locations may
   rearrange between visits.
3. **The Entities Watch** — NPCs and entities are aware of the player's fear
   level and react accordingly.

## Language

Dialogue mixes **Spanish** and **Nahuatl** loanwords. Environmental text may
appear in corrupted glyphs that partially resemble both. English subtitles
are available via accessibility options.
