# Atmosphere States Specification

## Overview

The Atmospheric Engine in Teotl V4 synchronizes its procedural soundscapes, dynamic lighting, and environmental fog with the active Nightmare Level (Tension 0.0 - 1.0).

This document outlines the atmospheric changes mapping to the five states.

## Core States

### 0 - DORMANT (Tension: 0.00 - 0.24)
- **Description**: Baseline environment. Safe and predictable.
- **Lighting**: Stable, natural-feeling lighting. Soft shadows, warm or neutral color temperatures.
- **Fog**: None or minimal (e.g., ground mist).
- **Audio**: Calm ambient tracks (wind, distant non-threatening wildlife). No tension layers.

### 1 - AWAKENING (Tension: 0.25 - 0.49)
- **Description**: The environment begins to feel unwelcoming.
- **Lighting**: Slightly cooler color temperature, shadows lengthen, occasional minor flicker in artificial light sources.
- **Fog**: Thin, slow-moving fog begins to form at the edges of the screen or low on the ground.
- **Audio**: A subtle, low-frequency drone is introduced. Ambient sounds become less frequent or slightly muffled.

### 2 - DREAD (Tension: 0.50 - 0.74)
- **Description**: Palpable tension. The environment feels actively hostile.
- **Lighting**: Shadows deepen significantly, starker contrast. Light sources may dim and slowly pulse or flicker noticeably.
- **Fog**: Fog density increases, obscuring distant background elements. It moves faster, perhaps swirling.
- **Audio**: The low drone intensifies. Occasional, unidentifiable sounds (creaks, distant thumps) are added to the ambient mix. Music shifts to a tense, unresolved chord progression.

### 3 - TERROR (Tension: 0.75 - 0.89)
- **Description**: Immediate threat. The atmosphere is overwhelming and chaotic.
- **Lighting**: Harsh, erratic lighting. Rapid flickering, unnatural color shifts (e.g., sickly greens or deep reds). Complete darkness may temporarily consume areas.
- **Fog**: Thick, oppressive fog that significantly limits visibility. Fast, turbulent movement.
- **Audio**: High-intensity, dissonant audio layers. Heartbeat sound effect becomes prominent and accelerates. Piercing stingers punctuate key events. Music is aggressive and chaotic.

### 4 - ABYSS (Tension: 0.90 - 1.00)
- **Description**: Total environmental breakdown. A surreal, terrifying climax.
- **Lighting**: Visual distortion, intense strobing, colors invert or bleed. Almost total darkness broken by blinding, unnatural flashes.
- **Fog**: The fog is all-consuming, perhaps taking on a dark, almost solid appearance, or completely replaced by a static void.
- **Audio**: Overwhelming noise. Distorted, deafening drones, chaotic screaming or synthetic screeching. Total loss of natural ambient sound.
